MY UPCOMING BOOK: BEAUTY BEHIND THE MADNESS

Eliana is a girl who tries to start over at a univeristy after a horrible high school experince and her parents divorce. With the help of her best friend Emily she finds new and adventuorous friends and even comes across a few enemies who despise her. Although Eliana is an innocent, sweet girl with family problems she tends to meet a so call "bad boy" Grayson who is very attractive and annoyingly rude but has problems of his own which he learns to ignore. As she starts to get to know him she comes to find out that they have more in common than differences. Can Eliana fix Grayson after learning his deepest secrets, and does he learn to fix her as well? Or do they find out that fixing each other is unrealistic? Does Eliana come to a conclusion that in order to love, she has to learn how to love herself first? Read more as we learn what the future holds for Blank and Grayson.
